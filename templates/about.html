About ❤️

Heart Predict AI is an intelligent web application designed to assist in the early detection of heart disease using machine learning technology. By analyzing user-provided health parameters, the system predicts the likelihood of heart disease with high accuracy using a Support Vector Machine (SVM) model.

This tool empowers users to make informed health decisions and promotes proactive heart care by offering a simple, accessible, and effective method for risk evaluation.

🎯 Our Goal

Our primary goal is to provide a fast, reliable, and user-friendly platform that helps users assess their heart health anytime, anywhere. We aim to support both patients and healthcare professionals with data-driven insights to enable early intervention and better outcomes.

💡 Our Capabilities

  • ✅ Accepts user health data through a clean and responsive form interface
  • 🤖 Uses a trained SVM model for accurate heart disease prediction
  • 📈 Displays prediction results instantly along with doctor recommendations
  • 📁 Allows users to view scan history and download detailed PDF reports
  • 📱 Responsive design suitable for mobile and desktop users
  • 🌓 Light/Dark mode support for accessibility and comfort
